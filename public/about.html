About Us

ProjectPlatter.com is a freelance website platform for recent grads, currently enrolled students in college, universities, graduate schools and professional freelancers to find coding, writing, design, consulting work online. The company is founded by grads from BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus, a premier university in India. The website is operated by Chief Executive Officer Siva, Chief Technical Officer Narasimha and Tech lead Suresh. The company is headquartered in Hyderabad, India and has users from all around the world.
